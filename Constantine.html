Constantine
Is a marine entity that used to live in Adez's realm, she was free to roam the bodies of water with the other sirens and mermaids.

However, when she was fined for trying to lure villagers to the ocean she was enraged and started using her powers to spread rumors and lies which lead to riots and crime, when her secret was found out Adez cursed her to forever be human, and banished her from the realm.


Occupation: Bandmember
