Devaraux
is a demigod that has the permission to visit different realms and pantheons.

However the more power he gained the more arrogant he became, he started to take over the realms instead of helping them. He came across Adez's, challenged her to a duel and was pronply beaten. Taking a huge hit to his pride he vowed to never battle again, believing in his head that he was inferior, but would never admit it out loud.
