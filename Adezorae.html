Adezorae, Adez for short.
Is an Eldritch being thats been around for an unknown amount of time, once she unlocked her powers she managed to create her own Realm using a Power Core as the center to give energy to all things.

However, some of her subjects started to spread rumors and convince others to riot against her, the Enemy took this as their chance to attack the Realm. Causing Adez to abandon the Realm and head to a neighboring world.
